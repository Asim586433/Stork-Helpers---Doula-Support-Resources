Our Story & Philosophy

8+ years of supporting families through evidence-based, compassionate care

Meet Tamara, Founder of Stork Helpers

After supporting her first birth in 2015, Tamara recognized a gap in the support system for expectant parents. What began as a passion project has grown into a high-authority doula brand serving thousands of families across the country.

With a background in both healthcare and holistic wellness, Tamara developed the "Supporting Character" philosophy: doulas as essential support actors in the birth story, empowering parents to be the protagonists of their own journeys.

Today, Stork Helpers combines clinical evidence with compassionate care, offering a full spectrum of services from pregnancy through postpartum. Our team of certified doulas, educators, and specialists are united by a commitment to evidence-based, personalized support.
